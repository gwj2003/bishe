Although the feeding ecology of sympatric anurans has been studied, less is known about the food habits of co-occurring invasive and native frog species, particularly in the Neotropics. We compared the food habits of invasive bullfrogs ( Lithobates catesbeianus ) and native thin-toed frogs ( Leptodactylus cf. latrans ) in a locality of Southeastern Brazil.
