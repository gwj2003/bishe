We investigated the effect of extraction temperature on the gel properties of gelatin from the skin of the American bullfrog (Rana catesbeiana) and the mechanisms. The textural and rheological properties of bullfrog gelatin extracted at 45 °C (G45), 55 °C (G55), and 65 °C (G65) were measured.
