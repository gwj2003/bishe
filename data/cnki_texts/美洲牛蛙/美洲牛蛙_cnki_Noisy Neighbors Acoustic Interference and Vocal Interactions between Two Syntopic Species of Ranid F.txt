American Bullfrogs ( Rana catesbeiana ) and Green Frogs ( Rana clamitans ) share ranges and breeding seasons, are ecologically similar, and often occupy the same breeding ponds throughout the summer. Males of both species use vocalizations to defend territories and attract females; however, Bullfrogs have longer calls and call much more frequently than Green Frogs.
