Abstract(#br)Immunohistochemical techniques were employed to investigate the distribution of orexin-A-like immunoreactivity in the bullfrog ( Rana catesbeiana ) pituitary. Orexin-A-immunoreactive cells were scattered throughout the pars distalis. We found that these cells corresponded to the cells immunostained with antiserum against bullfrog prolactin (fPRL).
