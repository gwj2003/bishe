Clinostomum is a cosmopolitan genus of trematodes that infect piscivorous birds, freshwater molluscs, freshwater fish and amphibians. Herein, a novel species of Clinostomum is described based on morphological and molecular data from an adult in the oral cavity of the great blue heron Ardea herodias and metacercariae collected from the gills and skin of American bullfrog tadpoles Rana catesbeiana.
