This report provides the first record of the exotic sucker fish P. pardalis from the Cauvery river system, Tamilnadu state, India. Information on the assumed presence of this invasive species based on sightings of a hitherto unknown species by local people and fishermen in stretches of Cauvery river have been confirmed by our study.
