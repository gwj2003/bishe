The aim of this study was to determine the effect of benzylpenicillin on the pharmacokinetics of acyclovir in red‐eared slider turtles ( Trachemys scripta elegans ). Six clinically healthy red‐eared slider turtles weighing 400 and 580 g were used for the study. Acyclovir (40 mg/kg) and benzylpenicillin (30 mg/kg) were administered intravenously to turtles.
