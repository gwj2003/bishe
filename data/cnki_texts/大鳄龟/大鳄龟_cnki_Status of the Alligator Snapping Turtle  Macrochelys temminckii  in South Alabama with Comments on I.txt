SOURCE_TITLE: Status of the Alligator Snapping Turtle ( Macrochelys temminckii ) in South Alabama with Comments on Its Distribution

Historic commercial collecting of the alligator snapping turtle ( Macrochelys temminckii ) appears to have severely reduced populations throughout much of its range. In this study, we analyze 8 seasons of turtle-trapping data from 11 major rivers in southern Alabama and provide the first report on the status of the species in the state.
