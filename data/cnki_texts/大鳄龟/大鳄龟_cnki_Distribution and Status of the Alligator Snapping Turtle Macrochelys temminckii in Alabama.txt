Macrochelys temminckii (Alligator Snapping Turtle) has experienced historical harvesting pressures that impact a number of current populations. Filling distribution gaps and obtaining demographic data can better inform conservation and management. We summarized multi-year samples targeting the Alligator Snapping Turtle in large river systems and major tributaries of Alabama.
