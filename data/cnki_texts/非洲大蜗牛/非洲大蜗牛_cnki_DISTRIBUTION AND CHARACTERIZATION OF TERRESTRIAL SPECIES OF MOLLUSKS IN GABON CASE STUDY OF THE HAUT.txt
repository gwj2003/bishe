The purpose of the study is to contribute to the acquisition of knowledge on mollusks in Gabon. This work was conducted to identify the different species of terrestrial mollusks in the Haut - Ogooué Province in Gabon. It was realized in the cities of Franceville, Moanda and Okondja.
