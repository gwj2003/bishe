The tropical invasive species Giant African Land Snail Lissachatina fulica is one of the worst of hundred invasive species in the world listed by IUCN. The snail severely affects agricultural and horticultural crops and home gardens throughout the tropics. The snail has reached India in 1847 and later it has spread into South India around the 1920s.
