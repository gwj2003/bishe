Introduction of alligator gar (Atractosteus spatula) into a new range of its distribution has been reported in several freshwater ecosystems. The presence of alligator gar in Indonesia has been recorded in Jakarta during big flood in 2007. The concern about introducing this species is because of its predatory behavior and can impact the trophic system in freshwater ecosystem in its non-native range.
