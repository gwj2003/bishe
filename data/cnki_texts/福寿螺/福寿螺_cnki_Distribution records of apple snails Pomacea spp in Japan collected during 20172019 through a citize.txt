Abstract Apple snails Pomacea canaliculata and Pomacea maculata are herbivorous freshwater gastropods native to South America and are introduced species widespread in western and southern Japan. Although they affect rice culture and are invasive to native ecosystems, high‐resolution distribution data are not available for these species.
