Pomacea canaliculata is an invasive species distributed globally, yet the mechanisms behind its rapid ecosystem dominance and lethal effects on native species remain unclear. This study analyzed the dissemination pattern of pathogens from the gut of P. canaliculata to the aquatic environment.
