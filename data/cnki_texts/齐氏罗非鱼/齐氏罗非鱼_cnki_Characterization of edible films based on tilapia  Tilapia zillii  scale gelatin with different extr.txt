AbstractGelatin was extracted from tilapia scales at pH ranges from 3 to 9, and the properties of resulting gelatin films were investigated. The ash and calcium contents in gelatin extracted at pH 3 was the highest compared to gelatin extracted at pH 5, 7 and 9.
